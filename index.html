How to use Warranty Tracker System

  1. If it has been determined that a warranty return is the only reasonable next step of escalation, send the customer a return label to their preffered email (if this is different than the one we have on file, update it).
  2. Click "Add New Warranty Claim" below, and begin to fill out the form. Enter the customer name as it appears in our records. Get a brief explanation of the issues the customer is experiencing, and also get the desired return address to send the product back to once the warranty process is concluded.
  3. Make sure to leave your initials at the end of the description in this manner:" --JGC-- ". Add the warranty claim to the board. All done!
